Introduction

Do you want to know how long it will take to pay off your credit card debt? Do you want to see how much interest you will pay over time? Do you want to compare different payment scenarios and find the best one for your budget?

If you answered yes to any of these questions, then you need our credit card calculator. This tool will help you understand your credit card payments and how they affect your financial situation. You can use it to plan your debt repayment strategy and achieve your financial goals.

The calculator is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and it also utilizes the popular JavaScript library jQuery.

Quick Start

Copy-paste the stylesheet link into your head before all other stylesheets to load our CSS.


<link href="css/Calc-style.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css">

Copy-paste the JavaScript & jQuery link into your body bottom all other after Html element.


<script src="js/jquery.js"></script>
<script src="js/Calc-script.js"></script>

Copy-paste the .ms-cl class into your body Element.

<html>
    <body>
        <div class="ms-cl">
            //write Your Html code
        </div>
    </body>
</html>

Features

Features
It allows you to enter your current credit card balance, interest rate, and minimum payment method.
It lets you choose between paying the minimum amount or a fixed amount per month.
It shows you how long it will take to pay off your balance and how much interest you will pay in total.
It displays a chart and a table with your payment schedule and balance history.
It helps you compare different scenarios and find the optimal payment plan for your situation.

The credit card Calculator's HTML structure consists of various sections and elements to handle different functionalities. The main sections include:

Customization

It is designed to provide a standard and reliable way of calculating your credit card payments and interest. However, you can adjust the inputs and see how the results change. You can also use the chart and the table to visualize your payment schedule and balance history.

Form Inputs

The following form inputs are available for the user to enter their credit card calculator details:

Inputs Description
Credit Card Balance - (#hm_in_1) The amount of money you owe on your credit card.
Interest rate - (#hm_in_2) The annual percentage rate that applies to your credit card balance.
Minimum payment method - (#hm_in_3) The formula used to calculate the lowest amount you can pay each month without incurring a penalty fee.
Payment schedule - (#pm_1, pm_2) The option to see the results based on either the minimum payment or a fixed payment of your choice.
Fixed payment - (#hm_in_4) A specific amount that you decide to pay each month, regardless of your balance or interest rate.

Additional Notes

  • It uses the average daily balance method to calculate the interest charges for each billing cycle.

  • It assumes that you make your payments on the same day each month and that there are no fees or penalties on your account.

  • It does not factor in the effects of compound interest, which means that the interest you pay is added to your principal balance and accrues more interest over time.

  • It does not account for any rewards or cash back that you may earn from using your credit card.

  • It is for educational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice.
