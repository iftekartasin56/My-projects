Introduction

Welcome to the Compound Interest Calculator, Compound interest is the interest that is added to the principal amount of an investment, so that the interest also earns interest from then on. This results in exponential growth of the invested money over time.

This Compound Interest Calculator is a user-friendly tool designed to quickly and accurately estimate the future value of an investment, taking into account the initial deposit, monthly contributions, investment time in years, estimated rate of return, and compound frequency. It provides a visual representation through a bar graph to easily comprehend the accumulation of interest over time.

The calculator is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and jquery library and Autonumeric Number Format and Highcharts.

Quick Start

Copy-paste the stylesheet link into your head before all other stylesheets to load our CSS.


<link href="css/Calc-style.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css">

Copy-paste the JavaScript link into your body bottom all other after Html element.


<script src="js/Calc-script.js"></script>

Copy-paste the #bmi__calculator id into your body Element.

<html>
    <body>
        <div class="ms-cl">
            //write Your Html code
        </div>
    </body>
</html>

Features

Features
It can calculate the future value of an investment, savings account with different compounding frequencies (daily, monthly, quarterly, yearly or continuous).
It can also solve for any of the other variables in the formula, such as principal, interest rate, or time, given the rest of the information.
It can provide a graphical representation of the growth of the investment over time, as well as a breakdown of the deposits, withdrawals, and interest earned or paid.
It can help you compare different investment options, plan your savings goals, and understand the power of compound interest.

How To Use (HTML Structure)

HTML Structure Description
.ms-cl The main container for the calculator.
.cl-wrapper The Wrapper containing the input fields and output sections.
.sp-rs-sec The section Displaying the Total Accrued Principal + Interest Result.
#graph_sec The section Displaying the Highcharts. A graphical representation of the balance over time will be shown in the chart

Form Inputs

This HTML document appears to be a Compound Interest Calculator, allowing users to input their height and weight to calculate their BMI.

Inputs Description
Initial Deposit - (#in_1) Enter the initial deposit amount in dollars.
Option - (.option-sec) Choose between Monthly and Annual contribution frequency options
Monthly Contribution / Annual Contribution - (#in_2) Enter the amount of money contributed either monthly or annually.
Investment Time in Years - (#in_3) Specify the duration of the investment in years.
Estimated Rate of Return - (#in_4) Enter the expected annual rate of return as a percentage.
Compound Frequency - (.option-sec) Choose the frequency of compounding - Daily or Weekly or Monthly or Annually.

Additional Notes

  • This calculator can help you compare different investment options, plan your savings goals, and understand the power of compound interest.

  • Compound interest is the interest that is added to the principal amount of an investment, so that the interest also earns interest from then on.

  • The compound frequency determines how often the interest is compounded. The more frequently the interest is compounded, the higher the future value of the investment.
