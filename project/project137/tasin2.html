Here are some tips to reduce your blood pressure.
  • Quit smoking. Besides damaging your lungs smoking constricts blood vessels and increases your blood pressure making it hard for your heart to beat.
  • Avoid alcohol consumption or have it only in moderation. Alcohol increases your blood pressure to unhealthy levels.
  • Follow a healthy diet. Avoid too much salt in food.
  • Lose weight. Obese people are more likely to suffer from high blood pressure.
  • Exercise regularly. It helps prevent High blood pressure and Diabetes. Besides it keeps you young both in your mind and body and is a great de-stressor.
  • Drink enough water daily.
  • Avoid stress.
