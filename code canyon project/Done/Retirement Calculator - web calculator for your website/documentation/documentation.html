Introduction

A Retirement Calculator is a tool that helps you plan for your retirement by estimating how much money you will have saved by the time you retire. It takes into account your current age, retirement age, existing investments, monthly contributions, and expected annual return. By using a Retirement Calculator, you can see how different factors affect your retirement savings and adjust your strategy accordingly. A Retirement Calculator can also show you the potential benefits of saving more or investing more wisely.

The calculator is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and Jquery library and Autonumeric number format.

Quick Start

Copy-paste the stylesheet link into your head before all other stylesheets to load our CSS.


<link href="css/Calc-style.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css">

Copy-paste the JavaScript link into your body bottom all other after Html element.


<script src="js/Calc-script.js"></script>

Copy-paste the #hm_cl6 id into your body Element.

<html>
    <body>
        <div class="ms-cl">
            //write Your Html code
        </div>
    </body>
</html>

Features

Features
It allows you to enter your current age, retirement age, existing investments, monthly contributions, and expected annual return.
It estimates your retirement savings based on your inputs and inflation rate.
It shows you the breakdown of your retirement savings into initial balance, contributions, and growth.
It gives you a suggestion on how much more you could save if you increased your monthly contribution.
It helps you plan for your retirement by showing you how much income you will need to replace in retirement and how much of it will come from Social Security benefits.

Form Inputs

This HTML document appears to be a Retirement Calculator

Inputs Description
Current Age - (#hm_cl6_in_1) Enter your current age. This helps estimate the time until your retirement.
Age you plan to retire - (#hm_cl6_in_2) Specify the age at which you intend to retire.
How much money do you currently have in retirement investments? - (#hm_cl6_in_3) Input the total amount you currently have saved in retirement investment accounts.
Do you get any equity based compensation? - (#hm_cl6_in_4) Indicate how much you plan to contribute to your retirement savings each month.
Do you have real estate investments? - (#hm_cl6_in_5) Estimate the average annual return on your investments.

Additional Notes

  • The calculator should take into account the rate of inflation as it can significantly impact the purchasing power of your retirement savings.

  • The calculator should consider the average annual return on your investments. This can greatly affect the growth of your retirement savings.

  • The calculator should factor in your estimated life expectancy to ensure you don’t outlive your savings.
