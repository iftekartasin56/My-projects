Introduction

A student loan refinancing calculator is a tool that helps you compare your current loan with a potential new loan. You can enter your current loan balance, interest rate, and remaining term, as well as the interest rate and term for a new loan. The calculator will show you how much you can save or spend by refinancing your student loan.

The calculator is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and it also utilizes the popular JavaScript library jQuery and autonumeric number format.

Quick Start

Copy-paste the stylesheet link into your head before all other stylesheets to load our CSS.


<link href="css/Calc-style.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css">

Copy-paste the JavaScript & jQuery link into your body bottom all other after Html element.


<script src="js/jquery.js"></script>
<script src="js/Calc-autonumeric.js"></script>
<script src="js/Calc-script.js"></script>

Copy-paste the .ms-cl class into your body Element.

<html>

Form Inputs

The following form inputs are available for the user to enter their Student Loan Refinancing calculator details:

Inputs Description
Student loan balance - (#hm_in_1) This is the amount of money you owe on your current student loans.
Average interest rate - (#hm_in_2) This is the weighted average of the interest rates on your current student loans.
Remaining loan term (yr) - (#hm_in_4) This is the number of years left until you pay off your current student loans.
New interest rate - (#hm_in_5) This is the interest rate you expect to get on your new loan if you refinance your student loans.
New loan term (years) - (#hm_in_7) This is the number of years you want to repay your new loan.

Additional Notes

  • This calculator is based on the assumption that you have a fixed interest rate on both your current and new loans. If you have a variable interest rate, your monthly payment and interest costs may change over time.

  • This calculator does not account for any fees or penalties that may apply to your current or new loans, such as origination fees, prepayment penalties, or late fees.

  • This calculator does not consider any tax implications of refinancing your student loans, such as the deductibility of student loan interest or the potential taxability of forgiven debt.

  • This calculator is for informational purposes only and does not constitute an offer or guarantee of refinancing.
