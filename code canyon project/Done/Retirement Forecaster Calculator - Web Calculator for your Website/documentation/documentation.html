Introduction

The Retirement Forecaster Calculator is a sophisticated tool designed to help individuals plan for their retirement by providing a detailed forecast of their financial readiness. By inputting personal financial information such as current age, desired retirement age, current income, and savings, the calculator projects the future value of retirement savings and the potential income available during retirement.

This tool is particularly useful for visualizing the growth of your savings over time and understanding how long your capital will last. It takes into account various factors like investment returns and inflation rates to give you a comprehensive outlook on your financial future. With this calculator, you can make informed decisions about your retirement planning, ensuring that you can maintain your desired lifestyle after you stop working.

The calculator is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and Jquery library and Autonumeric number format.

Quick Start

Copy-paste the stylesheet link into your head before all other stylesheets to load our CSS.


<link href="css/Calc-style.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css">

Copy-paste the JavaScript link into your body bottom all other after Html element.


<script src="js/Calc-script.js"></script>

Copy-paste the #mx_cl id into your body Element.

<html>
    <body>
        <div class="ms-cl">
            //write Your Html code
        </div>
    </body>
</html>

Features

Features
Users can input their current age and the age at which they plan to retire.
Users can input their current net after-tax monthly income.
Users can specify what percentage of their current income they aim to have at retirement.
Users can input the amount they contribute monthly towards their retirement funds.
Users can input an estimated annual return rate for their investments.
Users can input an estimated inflation rate. This rate is used to adjust both the monthly contributions pre-retirement and the income drawn post-retirement annually.
The calculator provides a graphical representation of the growth of savings over time based on the inputs provided.
The calculator displays various output results, including the projected value of savings at retirement, the salary at retirement, the age at which capital will run out, and the number of years after capital runs out.

Form Inputs

This HTML document appears to be a Retirement Forecaster Calculator.

Inputs Description
Current Age - (#in_cr_age) Input your current age. This is the age at which you're starting to plan for your retirement.
Retirement Age - (#in_rt_age) Input the age at which you plan to retire. This determines the time horizon for your retirement planning.
Current Income - (#in_cr_income) Input your current net after-tax monthly income. This serves as a baseline for estimating your retirement income needs.
Replacement Ratio - (#in_rep_ratio) Input the percentage of your current income you aim to have at retirement.
Current Value of Capital Savings - (#in_saved_am) Input the current value of all your investments. This includes any existing retirement savings or other investments you have.
Monthly Contributions Towards Retirement - (#in_mn_savings) Input the amount you contribute monthly towards your retirement funds.
Investment Return per annum (%) - (#in_inv_return) Input an estimated annual return rate for your investments. This helps project the growth of your retirement savings over time.
Inflation And Thus Escalation Rate (%) - (#in_inflation) Input an estimated inflation rate. This rate is used to adjust both the monthly contributions pre-retirement and the income drawn post-retirement annually.
