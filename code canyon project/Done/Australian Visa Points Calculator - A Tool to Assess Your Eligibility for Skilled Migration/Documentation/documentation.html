Introduction

Are you planning to migrate to Australia under the General Skilled Migration program? Do you want to know if you meet the eligibility criteria for the visa subclass you are applying for? Do you want to see how your skills, qualifications, and experience can earn you points towards your visa application?

If you answered yes to any of these questions, then you need our Australian visa point test calculator. This tool will help you assess your chances of obtaining a permanent residency (PR) visa in Australia. You can use it to calculate your points score based on various factors such as age, education, work experience, language proficiency, and more.

The calculator is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and Highcharts.

Quick Start

Copy-paste the stylesheet link into your head before all other stylesheets to load our CSS.


<link href="css/Calc-style.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css">

Copy-paste the JavaScript , jQuery & Highcharts link into your body bottom all other after Html element.

<script src="js/Calc-script.js"></script>
<script src="js/jquery.js"></script>
<script src="js/highcharts.js"></script>

Copy-paste the #mx_cl id into your body Element.

<html>
    <body>
        <div id="mx_cl">
            //write Your Html code
        </div>
    </body>
</html>
