Introduction

This budget calculator is a user-friendly tool designed to help you manage your finances by tracking your monthly income and expenses. You can input various sources of income, such as W2, 1099/Contract, Social Security, Pension, and Miscellaneous. You can also categorize and input your expenses, such as home mortgage/rent, equity line, insurance/tax, maintenance, HOA, and others. The budget summary section provides a detailed overview of your total income, expenses, and the difference between them for easy financial management. You can also download this summary as a PDF file for your convenience. This budget calculator is a simple and effective way to plan your budget and achieve your financial goals.

The calculator is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and Autonumeric Number Format & PDF Generate.

Quick Start

Copy-paste the stylesheet link into your head before all other stylesheets to load our CSS.


<link href="css/Calc-style.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css">

Copy-paste the JavaScript , jQuery & Highcharts link into your body bottom all other after Html element.

<script src="js/Calc-script.js"></script>
<script src="js/jquery.js"></script>
<script src="js/autonumeric.js"></script>
<script src="js/pdf.js"></script>

Copy-paste the #ms_cl id into your body Element.

<html>
    <body>
        <div id="ms_cl">
            //write Your Html code
        </div>
    </body>
</html>

How To Use (HTML Structure)

The Budget calculator HTML structure consists of various sections and elements to handle different functionalities. The main sections include:

HTML Structure Description
Income by Paycheck This section where you can input your monthly net income amounts from various sources including W2, 1099/Contract, Social Security, Pension, and Miscellaneous.
Expenses This section for you to choose an expense category and input amounts for Home, Automobile, Miscellaneous Loans, Utilities, Donations, Children, Eating Out, Clothing, Entertainment, Hobbies, Credit Cards, Savings, Medical, Insurance (not payroll deducted), Miscellaneous (list).
Budget Summary This section displays the total income and expenses. It breaks down the income sources and provides a total net income amount. It also shows total expenses and calculates the budget difference between total net income and total expenses.
Download PDF This button to save the budget summary.

Customization

This budget calculator is a simple tool that allows you to customize your income and expense categories according to your needs. You can edit, add, or delete any sub-category by clicking on the cell and typing in the new name or amount. You can also modify the month labels as needed, depending on when you are starting your budget.
