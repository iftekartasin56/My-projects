Introduction

Welcome to our Break Even Calculator, This Break Even Calculator helps you determine how many hours you need to work each month to cover your expenses based on either your hourly wage or your annual salary, taking into account any withholdings.

You can input your monthly expenses, hourly wage, and annual salary. Additionally, you can adjust the percentage of withholdings. After inputting these values, the calculator will compute the number of hours you need to work each month to break even.

The calculator is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript libray Jquery and Autonumeric number format.

Quick Start

Copy-paste the stylesheet link into your head before all other stylesheets to load our CSS.


<link href="css/Calc-style.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css">

Copy-paste the JavaScript link into your body bottom all other after Html element.


<script src="js/jquery.js"></script>
<script src="js/autonumeric.js"></script>
<script src="js/Calc-script.js"></script>

Copy-paste the .ms-cl class into your body Element.

<html>
    <body>
        <div class="ms-cl">
            //write Your Html code
        </div>
    </body>
</html>

Features

Features
You can input either your hourly wage or your annual salary, depending on your employment arrangement.
You can input your monthly expenses and adjust the percentage of withholdings to reflect your specific financial situation.
The calculator computes the number of hours you need to work each month to cover your expenses, based on your inputs.
As you input your data, the calculator dynamically updates the results.
The calculator presents results in an easy-to-understand format, showing you the number of hours you need to work each month and the corresponding number of workdays required to cover your expenses.
The layout is designed to be responsive, ensuring a seamless user experience across different devices and screen sizes.

How To Use (HTML Structure)

HTML Structure Description
