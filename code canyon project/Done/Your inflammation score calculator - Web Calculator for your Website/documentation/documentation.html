Introduction

Welcome to our Your inflammation score calculator, The Inflammation Score Calculator is a helpful tool designed to determine your potential inflammation level based on common symptoms and health indicators.

By answering a series of questions about your physical well-being and daily experiences, you can gain insight into how inflammation may affect your body.

This calculator focuses on various symptoms such as fatigue, weight management problems, joint pain, muscle stiffness, headaches and more.

The calculator is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript libray Jquery.

Quick Start

Copy-paste the stylesheet link into your head before all other stylesheets to load our CSS.


<link href="css/Calc-style.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css">

Copy-paste the JavaScript link into your body bottom all other after Html element.


<script src="js/jquery.js"></script>
<script src="js/autonumeric.js"></script>
<script src="js/Calc-script.js"></script>

Copy-paste the .ms-cl class into your body Element.

<html>
    <body>
        <div class="ms-cl">
            //write Your Html code
        </div>
    </body>
</html>

Features

Additional Notes

  • The Inflammation Score Calculator is designed to help users self-assess their potential inflammation levels based on a variety of common symptoms.

  • Each question in the calculator is associated with a score. A "Yes" answer typically indicates a higher likelihood of inflammation and is assigned a positive value, whereas a "No" answer is assigned a score of 0.

  • Low scores - indicate minimal inflammation, suggesting that the user's symptoms are less related to inflammation.

  • Medium score - suggests some level of inflammation, advises the user to monitor symptoms and consider lifestyle changes.

  • High score - indicates significant inflammation, recommending the user consult a healthcare professional for a comprehensive evaluation.
