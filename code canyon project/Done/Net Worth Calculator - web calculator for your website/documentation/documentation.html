Introduction

Welcome to our Net Worth Calculator, The Net Worth Calculator is a financial tool designed to help individuals assess their overall financial health by determining their net worth. Net worth is calculated by subtracting total liabilities (debts) from total assets (owned possessions and investments). This calculator provides a structured format for users to input their assets and liabilities across various categories, such as real estate, cash accounts, retirement accounts, debts like mortgages and loans, and other financial obligations.

The calculator is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript library Jquery and Autonumeric number format.

Quick Start

Copy-paste the stylesheet link into your head before all other stylesheets to load our CSS.


<link href="css/Calc-style.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css">

Copy-paste the JavaScript link into your body bottom all other after Html element.


<script src="js/Calc-script.js"></script>

Copy-paste the .ms-cl class into your body Element.

<html>
    <body>
        <div class="ms-cl">
            //write Your Html code
        </div>
    </body>
</html>

Features

Features
The calculator automatically calculates the total value of assets and liabilities and then subtracts the total liabilities from the total assets to provide the net worth.
As users input their asset and liability values, the net worth value is dynamically updated without the need to refresh the page.
The layout is organized into sections for assets and liabilities, making it easy for users to understand where to input their financial information.
The calculator is designed to be responsive, adjusting its layout and formatting based on the device being used, whether it's a desktop, tablet, or smartphone.

Form Inputs

This HTML document appears to be a Net Worth Calculator, allowing users to input their Net Worth.

Inputs Description
Real Estate Enter the total market value of your real estate properties.
Checking Accounts Input the current balances of your checking accounts.
Savings Accounts Add the total of all your savings accounts.
Retirement Accounts Include the accumulated value of any retirement accounts.
Autos Include you have any auto asset.
Other List the value of any additional assets you may have.
Mortgages Input the remaining balance on your mortgage loans.
Consumer Debt Include all consumer debt, such as credit card debt and store card balances.
Personal Loans Enter the total of any personal loans.
Student Loans If applicable, add the outstanding balance of your student loans.
Auto Loans Include any car loans you may have.
Other Debt Add any other debts not previously categorized.

Additional Notes

  • Your net worth can change frequently due to fluctuations in asset values and liabilities.

  • Ensure that the values you input for both assets and liabilities are as accurate as possible.

  • Use your net worth calculation as a tool for long-term financial planning.
